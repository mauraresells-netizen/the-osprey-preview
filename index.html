How It's Made

Three steps.
No shortcuts.

01

Source

We visit our farm partners in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Timor-Leste every harvest. No brokers, no mystery — just a direct relationship with the people who grow what we serve.

02

Roast

Small-batch drum roasting in Glasgow. Each origin gets its own profile, developed over weeks of cupping. We roast to highlight, never to hide.

03

Serve

Beans reach you within days of roasting, never weeks. Ground to order. Every variable — temperature, pressure, grind — dialled in and repeatable.

Roasting in Glasgow since 2019

Our Story

Rooted in craft,
open to all.

We began as a single roasting drum in a Finnieston railway arch. No investor deck, no brand strategy — just an obsession with where coffee comes from and what it can taste like when treated well.

We work directly with producers in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Timor-Leste. Every harvest is traceable to the farm. We roast to highlight the origin, not to mask it.

Come for the coffee. Stay because the chair is comfortable and no one will rush you.
